# BORN PINK World Tour: Record-Breaking Success

![Tour Photo](/images/BLACKPINK.jpg.webp)

BLACKPINK's BORN PINK World Tour has officially concluded, marking one of the most successful concert tours in K-pop history. Spanning over 36 cities across Asia, North America, Europe, Oceania, and the Middle East, the tour attracted over 1.8 million fans worldwide. The production showcased spectacular stage designs, cutting-edge visual effects, and unforgettable performances of both classic hits and recent releases.
